# 1. Project Overview

HerGuardian AI is an AI-powered web platform created to empower women through trusted knowledge, safety resources, health guidance, educational opportunities, career development, and inspirational content.

Instead of visiting multiple websites for different needs, women can access everything through one intelligent AI-powered platform.

The platform acts as a digital companion that provides personalized recommendations, trusted educational resources, and AI assistance while maintaining an elegant, accessible, and user-friendly experience.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Women often need to visit multiple platforms to access information related to:

- Health
- Nutrition
- Fitness
- Mental Wellness
- Menstrual Wellness
- Safety
- Career Opportunities
- Scholarships
- Motivation

The information available online is often:

- Scattered
- Time consuming to find
- Difficult to verify
- Not personalized
- Not globally accessible
- Not multilingual

This creates unnecessary barriers for women seeking trusted knowledge and opportunities.

---

# 3. Vision

To build a globally accessible AI-powered platform that empowers women by providing trusted knowledge, safety support, career opportunities, wellness guidance, and inspirational resources through one intelligent digital companion.

---

# 4. Mission

Our mission is to create an AI platform where every woman can learn, grow, stay safe, and discover opportunities regardless of her country, language, or background.

---

# 5. Objectives

The primary objectives of HerGuardian AI are:

- Promote women's health awareness.
- Improve access to trusted educational resources.
- Help women discover scholarships and career opportunities.
- Support mental wellness.
- Improve women's safety awareness.
- Encourage lifelong learning.
- Inspire women through global success stories.
- Make AI genuinely useful for everyday life.
- Support multilingual accessibility.
- Provide an elegant and inclusive user experience.

# 9. Functional Requirements

## User Registration

The platform shall allow users to create a profile using:

- Full Name
- Preferred Name
- Age
- Country

The platform shall remember the user's profile and should not request these details again on subsequent visits.

---

## Personalized Dashboard

The homepage shall greet users using their preferred name.

Example:

"Good Evening, Sakhi!"

The greeting shall change according to the time of day.

---

## AI Assistant

The AI assistant shall:

- Answer user questions.
- Personalize responses.
- Recommend trusted resources.
- Guide users across modules.
- Remember user preferences where appropriate.

The AI should be available throughout the platform, not only on a dedicated chat page.
